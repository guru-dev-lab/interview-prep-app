Xhire Desktop
Floating overlay for Zoom, Teams, and any meeting app.
macOS
Windows

Setup

1. Download and open the DMG
Drag Xhire to your Applications folder.
2. Run this command in Terminal
macOS blocks apps from unidentified developers. Run this once to fix it:
xattr -cr /Applications/Xhire.app
Click to select, then Cmd+C to copy
3. Open Xhire
Log in with your Xhire account. Pick a session. Click Go Live.
Requires: macOS 12 or later. System audio capture works best on macOS 14.2+ (Sonoma).
