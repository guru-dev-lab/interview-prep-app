Invisible to screen share

Content-protected window — interviewers can't see it even if you share your entire screen.

System audio capture

Captures interviewer audio directly from Zoom/Teams — no tab sharing or virtual audio drivers needed on macOS 14.2+.

Works with any app

Zoom, Teams, Webex, Google Meet desktop — any meeting app. Floats on top of fullscreen windows.

Synced with web canvas

Answers appear simultaneously on the desktop overlay, web canvas, and popout — all connected in real time.

How to use

1
Download and install — open the DMG (Mac) or run the installer (Windows).
2
Log in — use your Xhire account credentials. The overlay appears in your menu bar / system tray.
3
Pick a session — select the interview prep session you created on xhire.app.
4
Click Go Live — grant audio permissions, then the overlay captures system audio and starts detecting questions automatically.
5
Use during your interview — answers float on screen. Drag to reposition. Right-click the tray icon to adjust opacity or resize.
Requirements: macOS 14.2+ (Sonoma) for system audio capture, or macOS 12+ with screen sharing picker. Windows 10+ supported.
